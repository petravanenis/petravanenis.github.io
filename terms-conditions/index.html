In case of violation of these general conditions and house rules and in case of inappropriate behavior, we can deny and/or refuse you access to B&B Maaltebrug with immediate effect, without further notice or reason and without compensation of the stay costs.

Reservation and cancellation

Reservations can be made via the website www.maaltebrug.be or via the booking sites where B&B Maaltebrug is offered. Reservations can also be made by phone or e-mail. In that case we will send you a confirmation of the reservation. After payment of the deposit of your booking, your reservation is final. The balance of your booking must be paid one week before arrival or in cash upon arrival.

If you regret that you are unable to keep your booking, please inform us as soon as possible so that we can offer the free date to others. For cancellations within two weeks before the commencement date, 50% of the balance is due as a fee. For cancellations on the same day, 100% of the balance is due.

Access to the rooms

When making a reservation, the number of persons who will spend the night at B&B Maaltebrug must always be correctly stated. In case of an overnight stay with children, the age of the child (children) must already be indicated at the time of the reservation request. The rooms are suitable for a maximum occupancy of 2 persons. Visitors are not allowed in the rooms. Breakfast is reserved in the breakfast room at the agreed upon time. The private areas may not be entered by guests in the B&B.

If desired, this can also be daily. The cost of this is included in the price. The rates are exclusive of costs of cancellation and / or travel insurance and / or other costs. Rates of the B&B are always without obligation and are subject to change. All statements on our website are deemed to have been made in good faith and are subject to change. B&B Maaltebrug is not bound by obvious errors on its website. In the unlikely event that the service such as TV or wifi does not work during the stay at B&B Maaltebrug, this gives the customer no right to refund of the whole or part of the room price.

Check-in/check-out

In principle, check-in takes place between 16:00 and 18:00 hours. In consultation another time can be agreed upon. We always try to welcome you personally, but if you cannot be there on time, self check-in is also possible (we will then give you the code to enter). In any case, please let us know at least one day before your arrival what time you expect to arrive. It is also possible to arrive earlier and drop off your luggage.

Check out is possible until 11:00 am on the day of departure. After that you can leave your luggage for the rest of the day.

Smoking, alcohol and drugs

It is prohibited to smoke in the house/guest rooms of B&B Maaltebrug. Smoking is allowed on the terrace. There you will also find an ashtray to deposit cigarette butts.

Alcohol abuse is prohibited, so enjoy, but drink in moderation. It is at B&B Maaltebrug prohibited to possess, use and/or trade drugs.
